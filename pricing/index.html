Seshy Pricing

Seshy is a tool for both collaboration and storage.

It's always free to collaborate on projects.

You only pay for storage of projects that you own.

You can collaborate with others by creating teams and sharing projects with them.

Teams are free, and you can have as many as you need.

It doesn't cost you anything to contribute to projects that are owned by others.

Example Scenarios

Contributor

If a friend or band mate invites you to contribute to one of their projects it won't cost you anything.

You can download any projects that they share with you, and any changes you make will be automatically shared with them.

If you start a new project you can request to transfer it to your friend or band mate. If they accept the project then it will count towards their storage quota.

The Contributor plan comes with enouh storage that you should be able to have at least a couple of works-in-progress that haven't been handed off yet.

Solo Artist

As a solo artist you might be using Seshy primarily as a backup and archiving tool.

But you might want to work with some collaborators every now and then.

When you want to work with someone else, like a guest musician or a mix/master engineer, then you can create a team, invite them to the team, and then share projects with them.

When your collaborators make any changes to the sessions you've shared with them those changes will be automatically uploaded to Seshy.

Band

A band might use Seshy for a mix of collaboration and archiving.

You'd have one team that represents your core band. Any time someone starts a new project they can transfer ownership to the band team.

Then you might have another team that's the core band plus your mix/master engineer.

If you wanted to bring in a guest musician for just a track or two, you can create another team for that.

Producer

A producer might use Seshy for both collaboration and archiving.

You'd have teams for each artist that you're working with, or for each combination of artists who are all working together.
